CREATIVE COMMONS
GLOBAL SUMMIT 2015

The Global Summit is a gathering of experts, academics, and activists from around the world who are part of the Creative Commons network.
Held every two years in a different country, it aims to connect and strengthen this vibrant community. For the 2015 edition, the summit broadened its reach to include new organizations and individuals passionate about collaboration on projects that support the Commons,
open culture, and free knowledge.
If you're passionate about open access and knowledge sharing , whether you're a free software advocate, Wikipedian, librarian, lawyer, or part of an NGO, gallery, museum, or government, this summit is for you. Come be part of building a stronger, more connected commons.
